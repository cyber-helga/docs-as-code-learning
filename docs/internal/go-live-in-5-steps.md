# Go live in five steps

## About this article

This article explains how to easily get your first products live on a marketplace using ChannelEngine.

## Before you start

To streamline the process of connecting to ChannelEngine, make sure you have these ready before you can start selling.
  - Product data (in your PIM system, as a file, Google spreadsheet)
  - Marketplace account
  - Access to ChannelEngine
